Review: Attack on Titan Season 4

Our take on the final season of this epic anime series Attack On Titan has announced the release date for Season 4 Part 2. It is said to air on January 9, 2022, just as fans had previously predicted. Expectations are building, and even Hajime Isayama expressed his excitement at watching the show’s finale..

What Will Happen in Season 4 Part 2?

Currently, it has been revealed that Zeke and Eren plan to execute a plan that involves mass sterilization of all Eldians. Zeke had death surround him, until he was saved by a Pure Titan. Zeke’s death will lead to the failure of the plan. Season 1 ends with the Martians approaching Paradis and the Armored Titan, preparing to fight against the Attacking Titan. This war will have significant consequences. If Eren managed to defeat the Armored Titan, it would make him practically unstoppable. Also, the finale finale could show what can happen to Falco and Gabi, who have shown bravery and determination even as children.
